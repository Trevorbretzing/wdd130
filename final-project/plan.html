Content

Home page

Welcome to SHRDs official website!At SHRD we believe that sustainability and the ski community go hand in hand. Our main mission is to create high-quality and high-performance ski and snowboard gear that also has an environmentally positive impact. Whether you are a new skier experiencing the mountain for the first time or a high-performance pro-athlete SHRD is for YOU! Each piece of clothing is meticulously handcrafted using eco-friendly materials and sustainable manufacturing processes. The best part is that a portion of your purchase will go towards Forest preservation and wildfire prevention. Each and every single one of our products is created to be strong and durable but on the off chance that your SHRD apparel breaks tears or gets worn out, we offer free replacements so long as you return the jacket to us so we can recycle it properly. SHRD isn’t just about clothing it is about ensuring our environment and mountains are just as beautiful and pristine for future generations of little shredders!

With SHRD, you will be able to redefine what it means to ride responsibly. SHRD is driven by our purpose of creating environmentally friendly, yet stylish snowboarding apparel. The mountains we SHRED are in danger from toxic chemicals and microplastics being released into our atmosphere. When you SHRED with SHRD, you know you have done your part in protecting our environment. When you use SHRD, you know that what you are wearing is made from high-quality fabrics created from recycled polyester, organic cotton, and other eco-friendly materials. Although we cant create our product without having a carbon footprint, we promise to offset the carbon footprint of our operations by investing in projects that restore forests, promote renewable energy, and support other environmental initiatives. Our mission is to preserve these beautiful mountains we enjoy and love for the people of tomorrow. If you join us and ride SHRD, know that you have done your part in creating a more sustainable and environmentally friendly ski and snowboarding community. One way you can help us on our journey is by returning your damaged or worn jackets to us. In return, we will offer you a free replacement.
